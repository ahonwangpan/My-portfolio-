About us

Where silence
becomes image.

Ahon and Chachiia are a duo whose work lives at the intersection of documentary truth and visual poetry. Based between Guwahati and the high plains of Meghalaya, they travel with patience — waiting for the light that exists only once.

Their approach is quiet, unhurried, and deeply collaborative. Whether shooting editorial portraiture, landscape, or intimate weddings, every frame is an act of listening.
